? What is Pokébus?

It's a shared, co-op creature-collecting journey that lives in a Slack channel. There's no app to install and no game to launch — the adventure just happens in your workspace. One world clock moves the whole team from town to town on a tireless little Poké Bus. Hop in whenever you've got a minute: catch something in the grass, gang up on a wild on the road, challenge a teammate, then vote on where the bus rolls next.

🚌

One shared bus

One bus, one map — and the crew takes turns driving it through the region together.

🤝

Co-op by design

Tag-team wild battles, trade, rally the crew to drive, and vote the route together.

⏱️

Bite-sized

Drop in for 30 seconds between meetings — the bus waits at each stop until someone wants to drive.

1 You drive the bus

The bus doesn't drive itself — you do. It parks at each stop and waits until someone takes the wheel, then the crew piles on for the ride. Quiet when nobody's driving, alive when they are.

🅿️

3 What you can do

🌿

Catch 'em

Wade into the grass and a wild pops up right in a window — weaken it, then throw a ball. On the road, wilds appear in #wild-grass — join it to hunt, mute it to skip.

⚔️

Battle

A private, Gen-1-style turn loop — Fight / Switch / Item / Run with type hints. Challenge a teammate and it opens on a VS screen showing who you're up against.

⚖️

Always a fair fight

Wild levels track your team — and on the road, the average of who's aboard. Newbies and veterans both get a worthy, catchable fight. No one's bored, no one's walled.

🏁

Stop scoreboard

The bus roster shows how many each rider's caught at the current stop — a little friendly competition every leg.

🤜

Co-op the road

Wild HP is shared, so the whole crew can tag-team one tough Pokémon together — a real raid-team vibe.

🏟️

Gyms & badges

Gyms are the real skill-checks — “always open,” recommended-level hints, and a bonus for your first clear. Chase all 8 badges.

🚌

Take the wheel

When the bus is parked, drive it to the next stop — or hop on a ride that's already boarding. Riders are battle-available for the trip, too.

🔁

Trade

Swap Pokémon with a teammate through a safe, both-confirm trade window.

📘

Pokédex

Every species you see or catch fills your dex — seen vs caught, shinies and all.

🏆

Trophy Case

Badges, dex count, catch streak and your PvP record — the path is ephemeral, the achievements are forever.

🛣️

The open road

Travel legs are where the action is — roamers appear that you can only catch on the move.

4 All aboard the Poké Bus

When the 5-minute “all aboard” window closes, the bus rolls out for the voted destination. The travel card tracks your trip with a little bus driving toward the destination flag:
